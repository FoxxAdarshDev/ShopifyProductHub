<h2>EZBio® Ultra Bags, High-Performance, Customizable Solutions for Bioprocessing Applications</h2>
<div class="tab-content active" id="description">
<p>Foxx Life Sciences presents its high-performance Single-Use Bag Assembly, designed for efficient fluid storage and transfer in critical bioprocessing applications. These bags feature ports engineered for enhanced integrity optimized flow, that minimizes fluid hold up and provides reliable, repeatable and high-throughput performance. Constructed from medical-grade Renolit 9101 multilayer polyethylene film, they meet ISO and USP biocompatibility standards while offering low oxygen and CO² permeability and excellent low-temperature performance. <strong>Standard bags are offered in sizes from 50mL to 50L, with customization options for face and edge ports available for volumes up to 200L.</strong></p>
<p>All EZBio® Ultra Bags are available as made-to-order (MTO) assemblies, allowing you to select tubing brands and fittings with our standard bag sets. Each assembly is double-bagged and gamma-sterilized in the U.S. You can easily obtain a quote and place an order online or through a sales representative, with delivery typically within 4 to 6 weeks</p>
